Our Story

What began as a simple spark between two people quickly grew into something neither of us could ignore. From the very first conversation, Joseph felt an ease and warmth with Shelby that felt like coming home, and Shelby found in Joseph a steady, genuine presence she had always hoped to find.

Our connection deepened through shared laughs, holiday decorations, the love of our pets, late-night chats, and our dark addiction to antique shopping (we need help). Bit by bit, friendship turned into partnership, and partnership turned into the kind of love that makes life feel fuller, brighter, and more vibrant with every step we take.

Together, we’ve built a relationship filled with adventure, growth, and unwavering support for one another. And now, as we prepare to say “I do” in the timeless elegance of the Venus Gardens, we’re celebrating not just a wedding day, but the journey that brought us here. Join us for an elegant Roman Garden ceremony at the iconic Caesars Palace in Las Vegas—where we can lose our life savings, witness things we’ll never be able to explain, and fully embrace the true Vegas experience together. We’d be honored to have you be a part of this celebration!

Ceremony Venue

Our wedding will take place at the exquisite Venus Garden at the renowned Caesars Palace in Las Vegas, Nevada. This serene outdoor venue showcases lush, meticulously maintained landscaping, graceful Roman-inspired architecture, and an intimate ambiance framed by vibrant greenery and elegant stonework. Together, these elements create a peaceful, picturesque setting that lends a timeless and romantic atmosphere to our celebration.

We kindly invite guests to begin arriving at 5:00 PM to get settled and enjoy the garden before the ceremony. The wedding will begin promptly at 5:30 PM, and we encourage everyone to arrive on time to ensure a smooth and meaningful start to the celebration. A designated table will also be available for guests who wish to bring cards for the Bride and Groom.

Dining Experience

After the ceremony, we invite you to continue the celebration with an unforgettable culinary experience at the renowned Bacchanal Buffet at Caesars Palace. The Bacchanal Buffet presents a distinguished dining experience defined by indulgence and exceptional culinary artistry. Featuring an expansive array of globally inspired dishes, each prepared in open kitchens, guests are invited to savor premium seafood, expertly carved meats, handcrafted desserts, and exquisite seasonal offerings all thoughtfully curated to highlight both flavor and presentation.

Every station reflects meticulous attention to detail, creating an atmosphere of sophistication and warmth. Dining at the Bacchanal Buffet becomes a celebratory journey through carefully crafted cuisine, embodying the elegance and allure that make Las Vegas truly unforgettable. Each moment invites you to revel in the luxury, surrounded by exceptional flavors and impeccable presentation.

This dinner is completely optional, and there’s absolutely no expectation of participating — your comfort is what matters most to us. If you and your guests wish to extend the festivities over a beautifully curated buffet experience, we would be honored to have you join us.

Should you decide to join us, please make a reservation using the following link: https://www.caesars.com/caesars-palace/restaurants/bacchanal-buffet . Further information regarding menu offerings, beverages, and pricing is available on the website. We strongly recommend reserving in advance, as wait times can be quite long without one. The Bride and Groom are expected to finish photos around 6:30 PM, so a reservation between 6:45 PM and 7:30 PM would be ideal.

Late Night Festivities

The celebration continues into a vibrant night of unforgettable experiences. Beginning around 10 pm, we invite you to join us as the festivities continue in true Las Vegas fashion. Our first stop will be the electrifying OMNIA Nightclub at Caesars Palace, where world-class music, vibrant energy, and unforgettable ambiance set the tone for the night ahead.

From there, we’ll venture out to explore the iconic Las Vegas Strip, taking in its dazzling lights, famous landmarks, and historic sights. Whether you’re ready to dance, explore, or simply soak in the city’s magic, we can’t wait to share this unforgettable evening with you.

Accommodations & Activities

Guests are welcome to stay at any hotel that best suits their style and preferences. However, we recommend choosing a location on the Las Vegas Strip to enjoy the full experience the city has to offer. Since most of our wedding festivities will take place at Caesars Palace, staying nearby may be the most convenient option for your visit.

We will soon be sharing discount codes for room reservations at both Caesars Palace and Paris Las Vegas, offering convenient options for your stay. If you have any questions about these codes or need assistance with booking, please don’t hesitate to reach out.

Whether you’re looking for entertainment, relaxation, adventure, or a bit of classic Vegas spectacle, the city has it all.

If you’d like to plan a specific activity with us during your stay, please feel free to reach out, we’d love to share even more memories with you while you're in town.
